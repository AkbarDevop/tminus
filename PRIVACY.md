# Privacy Policy for tminus

Last updated: June 2026

tminus is a Chrome extension that replaces the new tab page with a countdown to
the deadlines you choose.

## What is stored

Your deadlines, quick links, logos, subtitle, and appearance settings are saved
locally in your own browser using `chrome.storage`. This data never leaves your
device and is never sent to us. tminus has no account system and no server.

## Network requests

tminus makes only two kinds of network request, both to display icons you chose:

- **Favicons.** When you open the settings panel, the extension loads small site
  icons for the links you added, from Google's public favicon service
  (`https://www.google.com/s2/favicons`). Only the domain of each link you added is
  included in that image request.
- **Logo image URLs.** If you pick a logo by pasting an image URL, your browser
  loads that image from the address you provided.

These are ordinary image requests made by your browser to show icons you selected.

## What is not collected

tminus does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. There
is no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing.
